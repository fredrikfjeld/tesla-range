Only change this if you know what you are doing.

Typical energy consumption is displayed as 200 Wh/km in the car, but real world tests have revealed it to differ from car to car.

It seems a typical energy consumption of 187 Wh/km is closer to the truth and is therefore a good default value. You can change it here if your car seems to have a different value.
